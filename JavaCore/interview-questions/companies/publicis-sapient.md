# Publicis Sapient Interview Questions

**Total Rounds:** 1 (2 hours)

---

## Questions

1. For given stream of numbers, find the median.

2. Separate odd and evens, keep evens at last with array.

3. Explain `this` pointer in Java.

4. Explain try-catch-finally, how do they operate, what all variations are possible with them.

5. What is immutability, how to create an immutable class?

6. Explain lock vs synchronized.

7. What is singleton pattern? Write code.

8. ExecutorFramework vs CompletableFuture.

9. What is Transactional objects/Proxy objects with JPA?

10. What is garbage collector, how does it work? How can we optimize it?

11. Have you used TreeSet anywhere, what is its internal structure?

12. Spy vs Mockito in Testing.
